About Pacific Trails Resort

About the team

Pacific Trails Resort started with a group of friends who wanted to help others reconnect with nature. They believed that people should not have to sacrifice comfort to immerse themselves in the great outdoors. Since then, we’ve attracted a team of enthusiastic nature lovers and thousands of happy campers. Our “family” here at Pacific Trails are experienced resort staff and wilderness experts excited to share their knowledge with you and show you the beauty of the North Coast.

Our misson

Spending time in green spaces makes people feel better, physically and mentally. At Pacific Trails, we strive to offer guests a way to experience the positive effects of nature without sacrificing comfort. Our resort is a space where you can disconnect from the rest of the world, relax, and truly find your zen. Whether you want to take a 5 mile hike, kayak out on the Pacific Ocean, or simply enjoy the views with good friends and a good drink, there is something for you to do at Pacific Trails.

Reach out!

Pacific Trails Resort welcomes anyone and everyone who wants to connect with nature. Please contact us if you have any questions regarding accomodations and accessibility options. We're committed to making your stay as comfortable and enjoyable as possible!
